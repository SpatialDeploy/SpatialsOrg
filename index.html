Spatials

Spatials is an open-source project aimed at defining a new term for 3D video. It treats volumetric, immersive video content as a distinct medium with its own standards and tools.

Our goal is to enable creators and developers to work with spatial videos seamlessly, bridging the gap between traditional 2D video and interactive 3D experiences.

File Format

We introduced the .splv format to support streamable spatial videos. This open format is optimized for 3D video content, enabling efficient storage and real-time streaming of volumetric data.

Encoders and decoders for .splv are available to integrate into your workflow, and our open-source players (SpatialJS and SpatialCS) support this format for playback on web and native platforms.
